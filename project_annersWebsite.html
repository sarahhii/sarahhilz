anners website

The
project

Over the course of two semesters, the magazine "anners" was created – If you want to find out more about the print version, have a look here.

Together with it goes an online version which offers all the magazine's content and even more.

In a small team of three students we planned, designed and programmed the website which goes along with the print magazine.

Compared to the print version, the website offers some additional content.

Some articles are available in longer versions or with additional information, others are available as audio files.

We divided the 17 articles and the additional pages among us team members. The design was roughly based on the layout of the magazine pages, but thanks to technical possibilities we had much more creative freedom than in the print version.

The pages were implemented with HTML, CSS, Javascript and Jquery.

All pages are responsive and were optimized for both, Web and Mobile.

The
result

The website was launched right after distribution of the print version and can be found here: www.anners-magazin.de/.
