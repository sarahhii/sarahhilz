Photography and photo editing is more a hobby which can come in handy.

My strengths lie in color grading, retouching and optimizing raw images to get the best out of them.

Most photos I took with a Sony α6000 and a Minolta MC Rokkor-PF 50mm 1.7 (MC/MD) (Adapter: K&F M15105 MD-NEX PRO) or a Sony α7iii and a tamron 28-75 f2.8. I edited them in Camera Raw, Lightoom Classic and/or Photoshop.
