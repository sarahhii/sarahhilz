Annual business materials

The
project

During my internship at Pirelli Germany I got the opportunity to set and redesign the annual business materials. The Materials are used throughout the year to present new products or company insights towards business and trading partner.

Pirellis needs were a print version as well as a digital version, which any sales representative of Pirelli could adjust to their own needs (e.g. deleting or adding country specific pages).

A brief
insight

The
result

I created the 78 pages long catalogue in InDesign. The result was a ready-to-print file, containing all necessary information, high quality images and crop marks.

Besides that, I created a PowerPoint file, which could be adjusted individually for digital presentations.
