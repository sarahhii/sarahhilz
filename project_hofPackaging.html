Happy Ocean Shrymps Food Packaging

The
project

Whilst my time as a working student at Happy Ocean Foods I had the opportunity to design the packaging of their first product to enter the retail market: Happy Ocean Shrymps.

The company wished to pursue a strongly branded, bright and colourful approach in their packaging that should straight away represent the brands identity: young and happy. Another important factor was the inclusion of waves and a playful use of font.

Together with the two founders and the Sr. Marketing Manager we discussed different design drafts I created until we decided on the final version. I then prepared the print-ready file with all cut and fold marks and handled the printing process.

The final result immediately catches the eye between other fish and seafood in the frozen or chilled aisle while at the same time delivering all USPs and product infos.
